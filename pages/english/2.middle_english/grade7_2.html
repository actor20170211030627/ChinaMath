speak Chinese 说汉语
speak French 说法语
May I speak to Lucy?

want to do sth. 想做某事
I want to join the art club.
I want to go to the museum next Saturday. 下周六我想去博物馆.
She does't want to watch TV now. 她现在不想看电视.

want sth. 想要某物
I want a new shirt. 我想要一件新衬衫.

want sb. to do sth 想要某人做某事
Our teacher wants Kate to answer the question.

join the army 参军
join the Party 入党
We want to join the English club.

join v.加入
attend /əˈtend/ v. 出席，参加, 随同，陪同；(比较正式的用法,例:婚礼)
take part in 参加(会议,群体活动,运动会), 参加活动

draw /drɔː/ v.画, 绘画(一般用 "铅笔,钢笔,蜡笔,水彩笔,粉笔" 画画)
drew /druː/ 过去式
drawn /drɔːn/ 过去分词
drawing /ˈdrɔːɪŋ/ n. 图画，素描画 v. 画；拉；吸引(draw 的现在分词)
paint n. 油漆，涂料 v.给……上油漆, 给……涂颜料(一般用 "颜料" 画画)
painting /ˈpeɪntɪŋ/ n. (一幅)画，油画
Can you draw?

I can't draw well. 我画不好.

You're very good at telling stories.
be good at = do well in 擅长于
She is good at drawing. 她擅长画画.
He is good at playing chess. 他擅长下国际象棋.
句型转换:
I do well in playing the guitar.
I am good at playing the guitar.

(2) tell stories 讲故事
Jim likes telling stories.

tell sb sth = tell sth to sb 告诉某人某事
tell me your name = tell your name to me.

tell sb (not) to do sth 告诉某人(不要)做某事
My mother often tells me to listen to the teacher carefully. 我妈妈经常告诉我要认真听老师讲课.

Write it on your heart that every day is the best of the year. ——Emerson

talk to/with sb 和某人说话
talk about sth 谈论某事

单词 短语 例句
talk talk to/with sb 和某人说话
talk about sth 谈论某事 		I want to talk to/with you.
say 后面直接跟要说的内容 He says, "I will go home."
speak speak to sb 和某人说话(比如电话用语)
speak English 说英语 		I can speak English.
spell /spel/ v. 拼写
注意: 不要和speak搞混淆了!!! 		Can you spell it, please?
你能拼一下吗?
tell tell sb sth 告诉某人某事
tell sb to do sth 告诉某人做某事 		I want to tell her the news.
